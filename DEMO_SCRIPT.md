## Demo Flow (5-7 minutes)

### 1. Introduction (30 seconds)

**Talking Points:**
- "This is Uprent Plus mobile - the fastest way to find rental properties in the Netherlands"
- "Built as an enhanced extension of Uprent.nl, focused on mobile-first experience"
- "Native iOS and Android app (not a web wrapper)"
- "Built in 20-24 hours to demonstrate mobile expansion feasibility"

**Show:**
- Welcome screen (if starting fresh)
- OR Dashboard (if pre-logged in)

---

### 2. Dashboard Overview (1 minute)

**Talking Points:**
- "Real-time overview of your rental search journey"
- "See your active searches, new matches, saved properties, and applications at a glance"
- "Activity feed shows live updates as new properties match your criteria"

**Actions:**
1. Show stats cards
   - "3 active searches"
   - "15 new matches" (highlight the number)
   - "5 saved properties"
   - "2 applications in progress"

2. Show quick actions
   - "One-tap access to key features"
   - Highlight "AI Letter Generator" as key feature

3. Scroll activity feed
   - "Real-time updates"
   - "Matches appear as soon as properties are found"

**Key Points:**
- ✅ Native mobile experience
- ✅ Real-time data sync
- ✅ Integrated with existing backend

---

### 3. Matches Feed (1.5 minutes) - **Key Feature**

**Talking Points:**
- "Our core differentiator: 15-second property alerts"
- "Faster than email, SMS, or any competitor"
- "AI-powered matching scores properties based on your preferences"

**Actions:**
1. Navigate to Matches tab
   - Scroll through properties
   - "Properties appear as soon as they match your criteria"

2. Show property card
   - "Beautiful property cards with image galleries"
   - "Match scores show compatibility percentage"
   - "One-tap save for later"

3. Tap property to open detail
   - "Full property information"
   - Swipe through images: "High-quality property photos"
   - Show match score badge: "85% match based on your preferences"
   - "All details in one place"

**Key Points:**
- ✅ 15-second alerts (vs. hourly/daily elsewhere)
- ✅ AI-powered matching
- ✅ Beautiful, native UI

---

### 4. AI Letter Generator (1.5-2 minutes) - **STAR FEATURE**

**Talking Points:**
- "This is our secret weapon - AI-powered application letters"
- "Generate personalized letters in 29 languages in seconds"
- "Saves 30 minutes per application"

**Actions:**
1. Tap "Generate Letter" from property detail
   - "Context is pre-filled from the property"
   - "Knows what property you're applying to"

2. Show form options
   - Language selector: "29 languages supported"
   - Tone selector: "Professional, Friendly, or Enthusiastic"
   - Length selector: "Short, Medium, or Long"
   - "Customize to match your style"

3. Generate letter
   - Tap "Generate"
   - Wait 5-10 seconds (show loading state)
   - "Generated in seconds, not minutes"

4. Show generated letter
   - "Professionally written, personalized content"
   - "Ready to send immediately"
   - "No editing needed, but you can customize if you want"

5. Show actions
   - Copy to clipboard: "One tap to copy"
   - Share: "Send via any app"
   - Email: "Directly to your email app"
   - "Regenerate with different options if needed"

**Key Points:**
- ✅ 29 languages
- ✅ Multiple tones and lengths
- ✅ 5-10 second generation time
- ✅ Ready to use immediately
- ✅ Saves 30+ minutes per application

## Questions to Anticipate

### Timeline Questions

**Q: "What's the timeline to production?"**
**A:** "TestFlight beta is ready now. App Store submission next week. Live in production in 2-3 weeks with proper testing and App Store review."

### Integration Questions

**Q: "How does this integrate with current Uprent?"**
**A:** "Uses the same backend API and database. No duplicate infrastructure needed. Can be white-labeled with Uprent branding. Shared user accounts and data."

**Q: "What's different from your web app?"**
**A:** "Mobile-first UX optimized for on-the-go usage, push notifications for instant alerts, native iOS/Android features, offline support, and camera integration for profile photos."

### Development Questions

**Q: "How long did this take to build?"**
**A:** "20-24 hours of focused development. This demonstrates the feasibility of mobile expansion using modern tools like React Native and Expo."

**Q: "What's the tech stack?"**
**A:** "React Native with Expo, TypeScript, Supabase for backend, same API as web app. Allows for rapid development while maintaining quality."

### Feature Questions

**Q: "Why is 15 seconds faster than competitors?"**
**A:** "Our scraper runs continuously and sends push notifications immediately when properties match. Competitors typically send emails on hourly or daily schedules."

**Q: "How accurate is the AI letter generator?"**
**A:** "Uses GPT-4 for high-quality, personalized letters. Generated content is professional and ready to use, though users can customize if needed."

### Business Questions

**Q: "What's the pricing model?"**
**A:** "Same as web app - Free, Basic (€14.99/month), and Premium (€24.99/month) tiers. Premium includes contract analysis and SMS notifications."

**Q: "Can this be white-labeled?"**
**A:** "Yes, the app can be white-labeled with Uprent branding. We can customize colors, logos, and branding while keeping the same functionality."

## Backup Plan

### If iPhone Demo Fails

1. **Switch to Simulator:**
   - "Let me show you on the Mac simulator"
   - Open simulator on Mac
   - Screen share or show on Mac screen

2. **Use Recorded Video:**
   - "I have a recorded demo ready"
   - Play `demo-materials/demo-video.mov`
   - Walk through while video plays

3. **Use Screenshots:**
   - "Let me show you screenshots of key features"
   - Navigate through screenshots
   - Explain each screen

### If App Crashes

1. **Quick Restart:**
   - "Let me restart the app quickly"
   - Close and reopen app
   - Login with demo account
   - Continue from where you left off

2. **Skip to Video:**
   - "I'll show you the recorded demo instead"
   - Play video
   - Continue presentation

3. **Show Screenshots:**
   - "Let me show you screenshots while we troubleshoot"
   - Continue with screenshots
